Randomize Your Group!

Thank you for taking the time to use my random person picker.
The concept of this application is simple:
  1. Add Names to the table.
  2. Click the Random Button to see who get's picked.
  3. Clear to generate a new table or Reset to go another round!
If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to me per the contact section at the bottom of the page.
Thanks again and Enjoy!
